LedgerZ

blockchain developers network

  • LedgerZ is an open culture, open source, non-profit blockchain and distributed ledger technologies developers community, running workshops with the mission of enabling developers to collaborating on learning, exchanging and to driving DLT scene.

    • We are doing deep-dive coding sessions, coding challenges, developer hangouts, discussion roundtables.
    • Our core aim is to together learn and exchange.
    • We support initiation of DLT projects with our partners from startups, institutions and other open source communities.
    • Our topics are chosen by the community based on the most interesting trends in blockchain and DLT development.
    • Our activities are funded and sponsored by partners.
